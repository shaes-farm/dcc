## Context

What forced the decision, and what the spec says today. Cite the section. If the
spec is silent, ambiguous, or contradicts itself, say which — that distinction is
the whole reason the record exists.

## Decision

What we are doing, stated as a rule someone can follow without reading the rest
of the file. Present tense, not "we will".

## Consequences

What this obligates, what it rules out, and what it costs. Include the parts
that are merely tolerable; an ADR listing only upsides is a sales pitch, and the
next reader needs to know what we accepted.

## Alternatives considered

Each one, and why not. The rejected options are what stop this decision from
being relitigated every six months.
